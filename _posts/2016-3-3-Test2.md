I use Facebook messenger a lot, and I rarely send SMS. The person with which I talk the most on Facebook is my girlfriend. During a period of three years, we have exchanged about 165 000 messages. As I am since a moment interested in "Big Data" or "Data Science" or ..., I thought 165 000 messages is a good data set to extract information from. I may look like a creep doing this, but it's not far from what Facebook is doing every day.

So I first looked for a way to fetch all my messages, and I found [this script](https://github.com/RaghavSood/FBMessageScraper). I fetched all the messages in about 10 minutes and wrote a script to import them into mongodb. Now I needed to think of a way to exploit this data set.

Conversations with my girlfriend aren't really high level. We basically say nice things to each other, "how was your day", "I love you", etc... Maybe an interesting thing to look at would be the amount of nice things we say to each other ? Like so, I should be able to see when things weren't going well, and when all was good in our relationship !

Okay so now that I know what I want, I need to determine what makes our relation go well. This is quite obvious. If our messages are cold and empty of tender words, maybe there is something wrong. The list of tender words/symbols I came up with is this one:


* <3
* taim
* je t'aime
* hihi
* rho

I'm from Belgium so we speak french. Basically, hihi is the equivalent of haha but for cute things. I counted the number of cute words exchanged for every month, and plotted the result.

![_config.yml]({{ site.baseurl }}/images/fb_data_mining1.png)

This is nice, but it doesn't really tells us if we were in full love mode or not. It just tells us when we are talking a lot, and when we aren't. We can see that during the summer vacation, july, aout, we are not talking a lot as we are either seeing each other, or in another country.

So let's replot a graph, but this time with the amount of tender words per messages exchanged.

![_config.yml]({{ site.baseurl }}/images/fb_data_mining2.png)

We can already deduce ideas about our relation from this. Unfortunately, it looks like three years is not enough to observe patterns. But we can definitely see how ou relation evolved and maybe speculate on how its state will be later.

Now let's separate the messages sent by me, and the messages sent by my girlfriend, and plot the two curves.

![_config.yml]({{ site.baseurl }}/images/fb_data_mining3.png)

The blue one is from messages sent by me, and the red one from messages sent by my girlfriend. It looks like the red curve is more stable, and in the beginning, is influenced by the behavior of the blue one. Without trying to force an interpretation, I can say that I am a bit more moody than my girlfriend.
